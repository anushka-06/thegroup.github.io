The Resort

Creating a lifestyle destination of this caliber is about more than just the thread count, wine list, and service. It's also about who you'll meet at the pool."

~ The Group

Following in the footsteps of the private hotels first popularized in London in the mid-'90s, The-Group Resort borrows from that sense of inclusion created by a members-only retreat.

Driven to a level of service on par with the most recognized luxury hotel brands in the world. Ours is a private enclave, without a name and designated by only a street number, because privacy and discretion go hand in hand with relaxation and retreat for our discerning members.

During the high season months, we present a rolling program of highly curated week-long events. With custom-tailored amenities and high-end guest services, members and their guests can delight in the sensual and boutique spaces.

Returning, time after time to West End Jamaica, secure in the knowledge that everything is taken care of with passion and dedication. The result is a highly crafted and curated open lifestyle experience, where members come to expect the unexpected touches of hospitality.

MEMBERSHIP IS NOT GUARANTEED
