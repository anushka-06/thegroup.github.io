JUST FOR DINNER

  • FOR YOU

    FOR YOU

    For those in the lifestyle who appreciate a slender ankle in a red-soled pump, the hand-stitched interior of a classic German automobile and a 2007 Insignia, this is for you.

  • five course experiences

    five course experiences

    Combining each chef's seasonal tasting menu with the sommelier's best pairings, these five course experiences are discreet, elegant and leisurely. They are also a perfect way to meet other professional couples, like yourselves, who are in the lifestyle.

  • THE PLUS

    THE PLUS

    The "plus" Dinners include the option of a deluxe hotel room or suite and the opportunity to linger after dinner with friends, both old and new, without the need to dash home.

  • Invitation only

    Invitation only

    Hosted at one of the best reviewed restaurants and held in the city's finest private dining rooms, the guest list is always truly selective and by invitation only.

  • THE ABOARD

    THE ABOARD

    The "Aboard" Dinners are hosted on privately owned yachts, typically in excess of 90-feet. These gracious evenings afloat are limited in size based on the vessel's Coast Guard certification and typically fill immediately once announced.

  • Michelin-rated

    Michelin-rated

    These Dinners are held at "Michelin-rated" 1, 2 and 3 star restaurants or may feature a "James Beard" chef with tasting menus with as many as 15-courses. The cost for these dining experiences will be higher than other dinners presented.

  • INFUSED

    INFUSED

    "Infused" Dinners provide the opportunity to share with friends the experience of enjoying THC and CBD infused dishes, all presented with culinary expertise, but with the guest's option to refrain from certain preparations.

  • A Personal Chef

    A Personal Chef

    A Personal Chef preparing a dinner in a unique private residence offers the most intimate of settings; however, the evening is still Just For Dinner.
