Going on Holiday

Taking time away, connecting with your partner, escaping the day-to-day cares of life is what vacations are all about. For some, that means being in the middle of where all the action is (The Resort). For others, the ideal setting is more secluded, the experience more individualized.

The Estate is located in West End, Jamaica, and while within walking distance of The Resort, it feels like a world apart. Originally the private retreat of one of Hollywood's most recognizable couples, The Estate is on another level. A higher level.

Two dozen private casitas, personal staff, dedicated chefs, and a tropical canopy to shade you from the outside world, all meld together to create your ideal respite.

Meals, activities, and excursions crafted to your personal tastes, create a level of service comparable to that delivered by the dedicated crew on The Yacht. Access to The Resort, its amenities, nightlife, and guests are just minutes away.

You choose.
