A Cut Above The Rest

Situated between the Estate and the Resort, lies a private enclave for the most discerning among us.

The Villas has its own dedicated staff of ten and a personal chef. The private grounds, gated entrance, and dock provide Members with a discreet way to arrive by car service, or via the Negril Aerodrome or seaplane. Of course, socializing with other members at both our nearby properties is welcome and encouraged.

Available for groups up to eight, the Villa's in-house concierge will take the burden of planning your ideal escape off your shoulders, so you may arrive knowing that everything has been crafted, down to the smallest detail, to meet your expectations.
